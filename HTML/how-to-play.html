How to Play Nonogram

Nonogram is also known as Picross or Griddlers. Nonograms are picture logic puzzles in which cells in a grid are colored or left blank depending on the hidden picture embedded in the grid. The grid contains numbers at the top of the grid and to the left of the grid, these numbers tell you if there are a group of consecutive black squares somewhere in that row or column. If there is more than one number in a row or column, it indicates that there are multiple groups of colored squares in that row or column separated by white spaces.
